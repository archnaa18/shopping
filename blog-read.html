Benefits of Home Delivery of Toilet Paper

Seriously – who wouldn’t want quality toilet paper delivered right to their door each month? With us, getting toilet paper is as simple as putting your pants on in the morning. Just choose a toilet paper package, register online and we will deliver it right to your door every month or at a regular interval of your choosing, much cheaper than what you would pay at the supermarket.


There is no hidden contract or binding commitments.

Simply
  • Choose the toilet paper that is right for your household
  • Register on line at: toiletpaperrus.com.au
  • Place your order
    You can purchase as a 1 time buyer just to try it
    OR
    You can subscribe for regular deliveries at an interval of your choosing
  • We deliver straight to your door

MORE SAVINGS

If you register as a subscriber for regular deliveries, we reward you by giving you a further 10% DISCOUNT off the regular price.

This means that we will send your order on the interval you have chosen.

We will also let you know a few days in advance that we are sending you the order so as you know when to expect it.

If you don’t need toilet paper, you can SKIP an interval scheduled delivery or simply CHNGE the frequency to suit your needs.

CHANGING YOUR INTERVAL SUBSCRIBED ORDER

Wish for to change the product & subscription or cancel you regular order?

It’s very simple.

Just log on, go to the “my account” section and click a couple buttons to what you want.

Job done! You control your orders with minimal fuss
